About Me

×

A love for art, A love for electronics, my love for restoration and creation, and a unspoken feud between me and my best friend all lead me to Software Development. While not knowing how the dots would connect, I began receiving subtle clues. I read an article about a Software Developer that taught a homeless man to code, and later created an app together, and began to envy them both. Later, a youtube link would reach me featuring Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerburg, Will-I-AM and others would reach me, will all of their messages explaining the importance of coding and how Software Engineers were "the new Rockstars". No longer able to fight the urge, I enrolled into Devry, and later Columbus State, to begin to gain the education I needed. A well placed online add explaining how software boot camps accelerated the learning and employment experience for adult professionals looking to learn software developer languages landed me into We Can Code It. While I still plan to complete my degree, my decision to attend this software boot camp was a great one. And with graduation weeks away, I am that much closer, to fulfilling my dream. If you want to know about the feud, feel free to ask me about it by accessing one of the contact links in the contact area. Thanks for reading!
